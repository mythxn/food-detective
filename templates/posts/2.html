THE FLYING BURGER

7.9
WITH BEEF, TURKEY, CHEESE AND FRIES
BUDGET BEEF HALAL

Burger Bros has become a restaurant that truly became part of Dubai's Deira since it's founding in 2001. It's located near by the metro station which enables easy access to yummy burgers for daily commuters passing by the area.

The burger is juicy and is greatly filling. It's fairly easy to eat the burger without making a mess. The sauces in this one give a great punch to the overall flavour of the burger. With a price-tag of 30 AED, it's great value for money.
