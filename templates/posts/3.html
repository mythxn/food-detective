PURI SABJI

8.2
TASTES JUST LIKE HOME
AFFORDABLE VEGAN DESI

Purani Dilli is where stories of fine taste and the fabled, buzzing by-lanes are relished together. Where nostalgia is served on a platter, entertained by the culture and charm of the most iconic historic capital of the world.

The Puri and Sabji combo by Purani Dilli is one of the most tastiest homely food I have had from UAE so far, the flavours pop in your mouth just like how mom used to make them. This is a place I will keep coming back over and over.
